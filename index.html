üìñ Project Description

CivicTrack is a Smart City Complaint Management System developed using the MERN stack. The main goal of this system is to provide a digital platform where citizens can easily report city-related issues such as potholes, garbage overflow, broken streetlights, water leakage, and public safety concerns.

Instead of manual complaint processes, this platform ensures a centralized, transparent, and efficient workflow between citizens and city authorities.

üéØ Problem Statement

In many cities, complaints are handled through offline methods or unorganized systems, which leads to:

  • Delayed issue resolution
  • Lack of tracking facilities
  • Poor communication between citizens and authorities
  • No accountability or transparency

CivicTrack is designed to solve these issues and improve civic governance through technology.

üë§ Citizen Side

üè¢ Admin / Authority Side

  • Complete digital complaint lifecycle is maintained.
